# Robotics Enters Its Mega-Round Era: $1.2B Raised in One Week

**Category:** Business
**Source:** [AI Funding Tracker](https://aifundingtracker.com/ai-startup-funding-news-today/)
**Date:** March 28, 2026

---

Mind Robotics, Rhoda AI, Sunday, and Oxa collectively raised over $1.2 billion in a single week, signaling that robotics has definitively entered the mega-funding era. Mind Robotics secured $500 million, Rhoda AI captured $450 million, Sunday hit unicorn status with $165 million, and Oxa raised $103 million. Robotics is no longer a niche venture category but rather an emerging competitor for capital alongside pure-play AI model companies. The acceleration reflects both a maturing market where investors understand the commercial potential and technological breakthroughs that have made practical robotics solutions viable. This capital wave will likely fund the next generation of hardware, software, and AI integration, pushing robotics adoption from enterprise pilots into broader manufacturing and logistics deployment.
