Adobe and NVIDIA Partner on Next-Gen Firefly Models and Agentic Workflows

Adobe March 28, 2026

Adobe and NVIDIA announced a strategic partnership to deliver the next generation of Firefly foundational models and build agentic creative workflows that integrate NVIDIA's compute infrastructure with Adobe's market-leading creative tools. The deal represents a natural convergence—NVIDIA provides the hardware and optimization expertise, while Adobe brings the user base, domain expertise, and applications that matter to professional content creators. Together, they're positioning themselves to define how generative AI reshapes creative work at the enterprise level. The partnership signals a broader industry trend where best-in-class infrastructure providers team with category leaders to create integrated, optimized solutions that are difficult for competitors to replicate. For creative professionals, the collaboration promises faster, more intelligent creative assistants while maintaining Adobe's control over model behavior and safety standards.
