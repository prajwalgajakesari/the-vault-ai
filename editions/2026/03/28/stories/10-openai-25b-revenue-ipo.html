OpenAI Crosses $25B Annualized Revenue, Eyes IPO

Fortune March 28, 2026

OpenAI has surpassed $25 billion in annualized revenue and is reportedly exploring a public listing as early as late 2026, marking the transition from private startup to publicly traded corporation. Anthropic is closing in at $19 billion in annualized revenue, creating genuine competition at the frontier. These figures move AI beyond speculation and into the territory of real, enterprise-scale businesses that generate measurable returns and represent meaningful economic impact. The race to IPO between the leading AI labs reflects investor confidence in the market's maturity and these companies' ability to sustain growth. A public offering would open these companies to deeper scrutiny around profitability, operational efficiency, and long-term strategy, while simultaneously validating the AI sector as a legitimate investment category for institutional capital.
