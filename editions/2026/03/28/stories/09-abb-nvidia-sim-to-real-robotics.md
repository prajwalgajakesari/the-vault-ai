# ABB and NVIDIA Close the Simulation-to-Reality Gap in Robotics

**Category:** Research
**Source:** [Crescendo AI](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)
**Date:** March 28, 2026

---

ABB Robotics and NVIDIA integrated Omniverse simulation libraries into ABB's RobotStudio platform, achieving up to 99% correlation between simulated and real-world robot behavior. The breakthrough enables manufacturers to design, test, and optimize robot workflows entirely in digital twin environments before any physical hardware commissioning. By eliminating the need for extensive physical prototypes, manufacturers can cut commissioning times by 80% and reduce associated costs by 40%. The partnership represents a inflection point for industrial robotics, where the convergence of simulation fidelity, digital twins, and AI-driven optimization makes factory automation orders of magnitude faster and cheaper to deploy. This advancement will likely accelerate the adoption of robotic solutions across mid-market manufacturing firms that previously lacked the expertise or capital for traditional robotics implementation.
