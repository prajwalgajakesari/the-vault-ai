# OpenAI's GPT-5.2 Pushes Context Window to 400K Tokens and Aces Math Benchmarks

OpenAI has released GPT-5.2, significantly advancing the company's flagship model with a quadrupled context window of 400,000 tokens. This enables the model to process entire books, long codebases, or extensive research documents in single requests.

GPT-5.2 achieved a perfect 100% score on AIME 2025 (American Invitational Mathematics Examination) without using external tools, matching Claude Opus 4.5's performance. The model scored 40.3% on FrontierMath, demonstrating strong performance on cutting-edge mathematical problems.

The extended 128K output token capability enables more comprehensive responses. GPT-5.2 Thinking mode shows improved reasoning capabilities, positioning OpenAI to maintain competitive advantage in mathematical reasoning and complex problem-solving tasks.
