# Amazon Alexa+ Gets Conversational Food Ordering via Uber Eats and Grubhub

Amazon has announced conversational food ordering for Alexa+, enabling users to order delivery from Uber Eats and Grubhub using natural language voice commands.

Rolled out on March 31, the feature allows users to describe what they want to eat, and Alexa+ interprets the request and handles ordering with minimal additional input. This mimics the experience of speaking with a restaurant waiter, reducing friction in the ordering process.

Available on Echo Show 8 and larger devices, the feature is included in the Alexa+ subscription at $19.99 per month. This expansion demonstrates Amazon's strategy to monetize voice AI through premium experiences and partnerships with major food delivery platforms.
