# Runway Pivots Beyond Video Models to Build an AI Application Platform

Runway has announced a strategic pivot beyond its core video generation products to build a comprehensive AI application platform and ecosystem. The company launched a $10 million venture fund to invest in startups building on its infrastructure.

Having raised $315 million at a $5.3 billion valuation, Runway is positioning itself as an ecosystem orchestrator rather than a single-product company. The $10 million fund signals investment in complementary technologies and applications.

Runway is offering 500,000 free API credits to developers building on its platform. This strategy mirrors successful platform companies like AWS and reflects Runway's ambition to become the foundational infrastructure for AI video and world model applications across industries.
