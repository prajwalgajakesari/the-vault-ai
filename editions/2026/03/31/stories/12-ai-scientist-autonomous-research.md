# 'The AI Scientist' Framework Enables Fully Autonomous Research From Idea to Paper

Sakana AI has developed 'The AI Scientist' framework, enabling fully autonomous AI systems to conduct original research from hypothesis generation through paper publication.

The system can formulate research questions, design experiments, analyze results, write papers, and submit them to peer-reviewed venues—all autonomously and at scale. Remarkably, the first paper generated by The AI Scientist passed peer review and was accepted for publication.

Operating costs average approximately $15 per paper, making it economical to explore numerous research directions. The framework has garnered over 13,000 GitHub stars, reflecting significant interest in autonomous research systems. This development raises important questions about the future of peer review, research productivity, and the role of humans in scientific discovery.
