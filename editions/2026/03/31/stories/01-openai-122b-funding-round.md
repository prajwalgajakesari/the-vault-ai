# OpenAI Closes Record-Breaking $122 Billion Funding Round at $852 Billion Valuation

OpenAI has achieved a historic milestone in venture capital, closing a record $122 billion funding round that values the artificial intelligence giant at $852 billion. Led by SoftBank, Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), and D.E. Shaw, this round represents the largest funding ever raised by an AI company.

The company generates $2 billion monthly revenue with 900 million+ weekly active users. Enterprise sales now account for 40% of total revenue, signaling strong commercial traction beyond consumer ChatGPT usage.

OpenAI is building a unified super-app combining all its capabilities. The company's IPO timeline targets Q4 2026, making it one of the most anticipated public offerings in technology history.
