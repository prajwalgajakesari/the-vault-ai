# Yann LeCun's AMI Labs Raises Record $1 Billion Seed Round to Build World Models

Turing Award-winning researcher Yann LeCun's Paris-based startup AMI Labs raised an unprecedented $1.03 billion in seed funding, making it Europe's largest seed round ever.

The company is focused on building world models—AI systems that learn to understand and predict the physical world. World models represent a paradigm shift in AI research, moving beyond pattern matching to causal understanding.

AMI Labs is valued at $3.5 billion post-funding and aims to develop foundational technologies for next-generation AI systems. The funding round demonstrates European investors' commitment to backing fundamental AI research and building indigenous AI champions.
