# Robotics Enters Mega-Round Era as $1.2 Billion Raised in a Single Week

The robotics sector is entering a mega-round era, with $1.2 billion in venture capital deployed to robotics startups in a single week. This represents a significant acceleration in investment into physical AI and robotics technology.

The broader venture capital market saw $297 billion deployed globally in Q1 2026, with robotics representing an increasingly significant allocation. Mind Robotics raised $500 million while Physical Intelligence reached an $11 billion valuation.

This capital influx signals investor confidence that robotics and physical AI systems are reaching inflection points in commercialization. Companies are building both the underlying AI models and the hardware infrastructure needed to deploy autonomous systems at scale.
