# OpenAI Pulls the Plug on Sora as Costs Hit $1 Million Per Day

OpenAI has shut down Sora, its much-hyped AI video generation tool, after just six months of operation. The shutdown reveals challenging unit economics in AI video generation.

Sora burned approximately $1 million per day in infrastructure and operating costs while generating only $2.1 million in lifetime revenue. User adoption peaked at 1 million but collapsed to 500,000 before the shutdown.

The Disney partnership worth $1 billion was dissolved with just one hour notice. Bill Peebles, lead researcher on Sora, called the economics completely unsustainable. The shutdown signals that AI companies must carefully manage product portfolios and focus resources on viable business models.
