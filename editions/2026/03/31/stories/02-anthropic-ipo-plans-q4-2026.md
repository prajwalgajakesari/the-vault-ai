# Anthropic Eyes Q4 2026 IPO as Major Banks Compete for Underwriting Roles

Anthropic has begun formal IPO discussions with Wall Street about a potential public offering as early as October 2026. Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan Chase, and Morgan Stanley are competing for the lead underwriting roles.

Wilson Sonsini has been engaged as lead IPO counsel. The company was valued at $380 billion following its $30 billion funding round, making it one of the most valuable private AI companies.

Founded in 2021 by former OpenAI researchers including Dario and Daniela Amodei, the company's potential IPO reflects the massive valuations commanding the AI sector. The race between OpenAI and Anthropic to go public demonstrates investor enthusiasm for AI leadership.
