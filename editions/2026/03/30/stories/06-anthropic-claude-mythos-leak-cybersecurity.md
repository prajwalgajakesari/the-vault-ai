# Anthropic's Claude Mythos Leaked: Most Powerful Model Ever Raises Cybersecurity Alarms

In one of the most ironic cybersecurity mishaps of 2026, Anthropic accidentally exposed details of Claude Mythos through an unsecured content management system. The leak, discovered on March 26, revealed approximately 3,000 internal files.

Claude Mythos represents what Anthropic describes as "a step change" in AI capabilities. Housed within a new model tier called Capybara, Mythos demonstrates dramatically higher performance on coding, academic reasoning, and cybersecurity tasks.

Anthropic's own assessment states that the model "presages an upcoming wave of models that can exploit vulnerabilities in ways that far outpace the efforts of defenders."

The leak sent cybersecurity stocks tumbling: CrowdStrike dropped 7%, Palo Alto Networks declined 6%, Zscaler fell 4.5%.

There is profound irony in the fact that a model designed with cybersecurity applications was revealed through basic infrastructure oversight.
