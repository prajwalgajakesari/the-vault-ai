# Agentic AI Goes Enterprise: Multi-Agent Systems Move from Lab to Production

In 2026, sophisticated multi-agent systems are graduating to production-grade enterprise environments. 72% of Global 2000 companies now operate AI agents beyond experimental testing, and Gartner projects 40% of enterprise applications will embed AI agents by year-end.

Yet fewer than 15% have successfully transitioned pilots into reliable production systems. "The biggest mistake is treating the demo path as the production path: unbounded tools, unmeasured quality, and no operational owner."

Customer service leads adoption. Financial operations report 30-50% acceleration in close processes. The autonomous AI agent market could reach $35-45 billion by 2030.

Companies that succeed will treat agents as infrastructure requiring governance, not features requiring only engineering.
