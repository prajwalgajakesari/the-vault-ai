CoreWeave Signs Multiyear Deal to Power Anthropic's Claude, Stock Surges 11%

CoreWeave infrastructure agreement positions GPU cloud provider as AI backbone.

CoreWeave announced a landmark multiyear agreement with Anthropic on April 10, 2026, sending its stock soaring 11% and capping off one of the most consequential weeks in the company's short history. The deal comes just 24 hours after CoreWeave secured a $21 billion expansion of its partnership with Meta, underscoring the explosive demand for GPU compute capacity.

Under the multiyear agreement, CoreWeave will provide Anthropic with cloud computing capacity needed to build and deploy Claude. Market analysts have pegged the value at approximately $6.8 billion over the contract period, making it one of the largest single infrastructure commitments in the AI industry.

"We're thrilled to be working with Anthropic to power the next generation of Claude," said a CoreWeave spokesperson. The announcement reflects a seismic shift in how AI companies approach compute infrastructure, moving from managing their own data centers to outsourcing to specialized providers.

CoreWeave's stock price jumped from $92 to $103 on the day of the announcement. By the close of trading Friday, CoreWeave was up 29.3% for the week. With the addition of Anthropic, CoreWeave now counts nine of the ten leading AI model providers as customers.

CoreWeave's backlog now exceeds $66.8 billion. The company has guided 2026 revenue of $12 to $13 billion. At a market capitalization of $54 billion, CoreWeave remains relatively small compared to the mega-cap tech stocks, yet its growth rate positions it as one of the fastest-growing infrastructure companies in the tech sector.

The CoreWeave-Anthropic deal is emblematic of a larger truth: AI infrastructure is becoming the most critical constraint in artificial intelligence development. Model architectures are converging, training techniques are well-understood, but securing enough GPU capacity to train and run models at scale remains extraordinarily difficult. For Anthropic, the CoreWeave partnership removes a significant operational constraint.
