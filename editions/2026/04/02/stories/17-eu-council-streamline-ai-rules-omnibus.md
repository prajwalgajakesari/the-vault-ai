The EU Council reached agreement on the Omnibus VII package that streamlines existing AI regulations and reduces compliance complexity for European companies. The revised framework maintains safety standards while eliminating redundant requirements across overlapping regulations. The streamlining is expected to accelerate European AI development and make the region more competitive globally. This policy shift reflects balancing regulatory rigor with innovation encouragement.
