Poolside AI is in negotiations with Google to revive a 2-gigawatt Texas data center project following the collapse of its CoreWeave partnership deal. The facility is critical for supporting large-scale AI inference and training workloads in North America. Google partnership would provide the capital and operational expertise needed to bring the facility online. This development highlights the intense competition for AI infrastructure capacity in the US market.
