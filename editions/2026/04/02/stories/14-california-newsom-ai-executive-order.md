California Governor Newsom issued an executive order establishing mandatory AI safety requirements for all state government contracts. The order sets new standards for algorithmic transparency, bias testing, and accountability in AI systems used by state agencies. Any AI system procurement by California must undergo rigorous safety and fairness evaluations. This policy positions California as a leader in government AI governance and sets precedent for other states.
