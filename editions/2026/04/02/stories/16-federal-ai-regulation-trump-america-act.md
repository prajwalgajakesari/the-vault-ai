The Trump administration introduced the TRUMP AMERICA AI Act alongside a comprehensive White House AI regulatory framework. The legislation aims to position the United States as a leader in AI innovation while establishing guardrails for high-risk applications. The framework coordinates between federal agencies and sets standards for AI transparency and accountability. This represents a significant federal commitment to shaping AI development within US borders.
