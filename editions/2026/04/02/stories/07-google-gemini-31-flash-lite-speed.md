Google released Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite, a streamlined version of its Gemini model offering 2.5x faster inference while costing only \$0.25 per million tokens. This dramatic improvement in cost-efficiency makes high-quality AI more accessible to developers and enterprises. Flash-Lite maintains competitive quality while substantially reducing computational requirements. The pricing represents a shift in AI economics that may reshape market competition.
