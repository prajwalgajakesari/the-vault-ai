Researchers developed an AI model capable of diagnosing coronary microvascular dysfunction from a standard 10-second electrocardiogram, achieving 92% diagnostic accuracy. This advancement enables earlier detection of a condition that affects millions of heart disease patients globally. The AI approach makes diagnosis accessible without requiring expensive imaging procedures. The breakthrough could transform cardiac care protocols and improve patient outcomes significantly.
