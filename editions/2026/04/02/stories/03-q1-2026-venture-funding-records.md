Q1 2026 has set unprecedented venture capital records with \$300 billion invested globally, with artificial intelligence companies capturing 80% of all funding. This represents a dramatic shift in capital allocation toward AI-focused startups and infrastructure. The concentration of funding in AI reflects investor expectations for transformative returns in the coming decade. Traditional tech sectors are seeing reduced investment as capital gravitates toward AI innovation.
