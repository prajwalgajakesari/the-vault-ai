AWS announced Strands Labs, a new division dedicated to experimental AI agent research and development spanning robotics, software automation, and industrial applications. The lab focuses on pushing boundaries of what autonomous AI agents can accomplish across physical and digital domains. Strands Labs partners with academic institutions and enterprises for collaborative R&D. This initiative positions AWS as a key player in advancing agent-based AI technology.
