Tennessee became the first state to sign legislation banning AI-powered therapy bots from providing mental health treatment without human oversight. The law reflects growing concerns about AI limitations in addressing complex psychological conditions. Other states are following Tennessee's lead with similar mental health AI restrictions. This regulatory trend signals public and legislative skepticism about replacing human clinicians with autonomous AI systems.
