Intel has repurchased a significant stake in its Ireland Fab 34 facility from Apollo Global Management for \$14.2 billion, reaffirming its commitment to semiconductor manufacturing capacity. The stock market responded positively with a 9% surge following the announcement. This strategic move strengthens Intel's control over critical chip production infrastructure in Europe. The transaction signals Intel's confidence in the long-term viability of advanced semiconductor manufacturing.
